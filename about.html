About Elna Construction Materials PLC

Building Ethiopia's future with quality materials since 2010

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Elna Construction Materials PLC began as a small family-owned business in Addis Ababa with a vision to provide quality construction materials to Ethiopia's growing construction industry.

Over the years, we have grown into one of the leading suppliers of construction materials in Ethiopia, expanding our product range to include steel, ceramic tiles, and industrial hygiene products.

Today, we serve clients across Ethiopia, from individual homeowners to large-scale contractors and government projects, always maintaining our commitment to quality and customer service.

Our Mission

To support Ethiopia's infrastructure development by providing high-quality, certified construction materials that meet international standards while being locally accessible and affordable.

Our Vision

To be Ethiopia's most trusted and reliable construction materials supplier, recognized for quality, integrity, and commitment to national development.
